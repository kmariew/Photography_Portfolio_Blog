Hello,Nice to meet you.

I started Hourglass Photography when I realized I never wanted to put my camera down. I love exploring and how through photography I’m able to capture moments as they happen. Raw emotion and authentic feelings are what I live to photograph.

Nothing can convey meaning like a photograph can. I am excited about every project because they are all unique and have their own message, whether it’s the architectural design and feeling of a residence or happy-excited tears on your wedding day.
